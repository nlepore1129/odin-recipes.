Kinsleys Birthday Cake

this is the cake Kinsley likes to have on her birthdays

Ingredients

  • cake batter
  • sprinkles dots
  • frosting
  • colorful candels
  • eggs

Steps

  1. mix cake batter with eggs
  2. bake in oven at 350 degrees for 30 min
  3. let cake cool then add frosting
  4. add candels
  5. sing happy birthday
  6. eat the cake
