Pizza

Tastiest Za ever!

My kids absolutley love this pizza recipe, they ask me at least once a week to make it.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Tomato Sauce
  • shredded Cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. roll dough out
  2. spread tomato sauce
  3. toss cheese on
  4. add pepperoni
  5. put in oven at 350 for 25 minutes
